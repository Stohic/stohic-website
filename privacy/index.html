Privacy Policy

Stohic, Inc.
Effective Date: April 3, 2026

Stohic, Inc. ("Stohic," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Enclave secure collaboration platform ("Enclave" or the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Enclave iOS application and related services.

1. Architecture and Data Isolation

Each Enclave customer receives a dedicated, isolated deployment on its own infrastructure. Your organization's data is never co-located with, shared with, or accessible to any other organization. Stohic does not operate a shared multi-tenant environment.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When your organization provisions your Enclave account, the following information is stored within your organization's dedicated instance:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • User ID and device identifiers

Content You Create

All content you create within Enclave is stored exclusively within your organization's isolated deployment:

  • Chat messages and threads
  • Files and documents you upload
  • Notes and bulletins
  • Tasks and calendar events
  • Contacts and address book entries
  • Secrets stored in the secrets manager

Device and Technical Information

To provide the Service, we collect:

  • Device identifiers (for session management and push notifications)
  • Push notification tokens (if you enable notifications)
  • Authentication tokens (stored securely on your device)

Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID authentication, biometric verification is handled entirely by your device's operating system. Stohic never receives, processes, or stores your biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Provide and operate the Enclave collaboration features
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your session
  • Deliver push notifications you have opted into
  • Maintain the security and integrity of your organization's deployment

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or ad targeting
  • Cross-app tracking
  • Selling or sharing data with third parties
  • Analytics or profiling beyond what is necessary to operate the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Each customer deployment runs on dedicated, isolated AWS infrastructure
  • Authentication supports biometrics, passkeys, and multi-factor authentication
  • WebSocket connections use secure protocols (WSS) exclusively in production
  • Session tokens are stored in secure device storage (iOS Keychain via Expo SecureStore)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data may only be disclosed:

  • To your organization's administrators, as configured within your Enclave instance
  • If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Stohic, our users, or the public

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained within your organization's dedicated Enclave deployment for as long as your organization maintains an active subscription. Your organization's administrators control data retention policies within their instance.

Upon termination of service, your organization's entire deployment and all associated data is permanently destroyed.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To exercise these rights, contact your organization's Enclave administrator or reach out to us directly.

8. Children's Privacy

Enclave is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your organization's Enclave deployment is hosted in the AWS region selected during provisioning. Data remains within that region unless your organization's administrators configure otherwise.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify affected customers of material changes through the Service or via email. The "Effective Date" at the top of this policy indicates the date of the most recent revision.
